What we do

Pianotramp is a centralised platform for peer-to-peer piano booking. It is the easiest way to find a piano to practice, wherever you are and an any moment.

Put your own piano for rent in less than one minute

It takes less time than a Bach praeludium to create your account, set your pricing and schedule and give the possibility to other people the fantastic opportunity to practice on your own piano !

Visualise all the available pianos

You are travelling and desesperate to find a piano to practice ? Don't despair ! Pianotramp gives you a clear view of the available pianos around you sorted by date, brand, type and location.

Fast payment

Direct payment with Paypal, 100% secured and real time.

About
